Waking Up with the Warrens

The Warren Family stands smiling for their Christmas card photo.

John is the father of the household. He is a Colorado native through and through! He works as a financial advisor for Northwestern Mutual in the Denver area.

Lisa is the mother of the Warren family. Lisa is a wild go-getter and is always in the middle of a project. She is the definition of "SUPERMOM".

Lily is the eldest Warren child. She is 20 years old an is attending Westmont College in Santa Barbara, CA.

Chloe is the second oldest child. She is 18 years old and loves her Colorado roots and her family!

Sophie is the sporty 16 year-old. She loves to be active and play with her puppy. She is always the life of the party!

Samuel is the next child in the lineup. He is 11 years old and loves to have a good time! He loves football and helping his dad out with the power-tools. Sam is such a sweetheart and always has a smile on his face.

Phoebe is the crazy red-head. She is just a ball of energy and love! She always makes people around her happy just by her exciting spirit.

Finally, we have Benjamin. Ben is the youngest Warren coming in at 5 years old. He is just like his dad in everyway. He cares deeply about people and just wants to love on them. He is also arguably one of the smartest of the bunch. He acts muc more mature than a normal 5 year old should.
